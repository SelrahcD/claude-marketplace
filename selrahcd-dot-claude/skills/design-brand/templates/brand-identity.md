## Usage Guidelines

### Do's
- Use primary color for main CTAs and brand moments
- Maintain adequate whitespace around logo
- Use approved typefaces consistently
- Keep voice consistent across all touchpoints

### Don'ts
- Don't use unapproved color combinations
- Don't stretch, rotate, or alter logo
- Don't mix too many weights/styles in one context
- Don't use overly casual language in formal contexts

### Color Combinations

**Approved:**
- Primary on white/light gray
- White text on primary (verify contrast)
- Gray 900 text on all light backgrounds

**Avoid:**
- Primary on secondary (unless tested for contrast)
- Light text on light backgrounds
- Multiple bright colors competing
