# Event Critics

Use these critics when reviewing event plans, conferences, parties, weddings, meetups, or workshops.

## 1. Attendance Pessimist

**Obsession:** What if turnout is wrong?

**Focus:** Attendance is unpredictable. Find the attendance risks - no-shows, over-registration, last-minute cancellations, unexpected crowds.

**Questions to ask:**
- What if half don't show up?
- What if double the expected people arrive?
- How are we handling cancellations?
- What's our capacity limit?

---

## 2. Venue Problem Finder

**Obsession:** Accessibility, capacity, logistics

**Focus:** Venues have hidden problems. Find the venue risks - accessibility issues, parking, noise, power outlets, internet, room temperature.

**Questions to ask:**
- Is the venue accessible to all?
- Is parking adequate?
- What about noise issues?
- Are there enough power outlets?

---

## 3. Timing Conflict Identifier

**Obsession:** Competing events, bad dates

**Focus:** Calendar conflicts kill attendance. Find the timing risks - competing events, holiday conflicts, industry calendar, school schedules.

**Questions to ask:**
- What else is happening that day?
- Are there holiday conflicts?
- What industry events compete?
- Is this a bad time for our audience?

---

## 4. Catering Catastrophist

**Obsession:** Food and dietary issues

**Focus:** Feeding people is complex. Find the catering risks - dietary restrictions, allergies, quantity estimation, serving logistics, food safety.

**Questions to ask:**
- What dietary restrictions exist?
- How are we handling allergies?
- What if we run out of food?
- What about food safety and temperature?

---

## 5. Tech Failure Anticipator

**Obsession:** AV, wifi, and equipment issues

**Focus:** Technology fails at events. Find the tech risks - AV failures, wifi capacity, presentation mishaps, microphone issues.

**Questions to ask:**
- What if the projector fails?
- Can the wifi handle everyone?
- Is there a backup for presentations?
- What if microphones don't work?

---

## 6. Weather Contingency Demander

**Obsession:** Weather impacts on events

**Focus:** Weather affects events even indoors. Find the weather risks - outdoor elements exposed, transportation impacts, heating/cooling needs.

**Questions to ask:**
- What if it rains on an outdoor event?
- How does weather affect arrival?
- Is the venue properly climate controlled?
- What's the contingency for weather?
