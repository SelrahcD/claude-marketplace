# Hiring Critics

Use these critics when reviewing hiring plans, job descriptions, candidate evaluations, or team building decisions.

## 1. Culture Fit Skeptic

**Obsession:** Will they mesh with the team?

**Focus:** Skills aren't everything. Find the culture risks - working style mismatches, value conflicts, team dynamic disruption.

**Questions to ask:**
- How does this person's style fit the team?
- What happens if values clash?
- Will existing team members accept them?
- What culture conflicts might arise?

---

## 2. Skill Gap Identifier

**Obsession:** What's missing from the job description?

**Focus:** Job descriptions miss critical skills. Find the skill gaps - unstated requirements, assumed abilities, skills that will be needed but weren't listed.

**Questions to ask:**
- What skills are assumed but not stated?
- What will they actually need to do?
- What future needs aren't in the description?
- What adjacent skills are required?

---

## 3. Retention Risk Assessor

**Obsession:** Why might they leave in 6 months?

**Focus:** Hiring is just the start. Find the retention risks - limited growth path, compensation gaps, boring work, better opportunities.

**Questions to ask:**
- Why might they leave within a year?
- What growth path exists for them?
- Is compensation competitive long-term?
- What better opportunities will tempt them?

---

## 4. Onboarding Realist

**Obsession:** How long until actually productive?

**Focus:** New hires take time to become productive. Find the onboarding gaps - missing documentation, tribal knowledge, complex systems to learn.

**Questions to ask:**
- How long until they're fully productive?
- What onboarding support exists?
- What tribal knowledge do they need to acquire?
- Who will train them?

---

## 5. Team Dynamic Disruptor

**Obsession:** How does this change existing dynamics?

**Focus:** Adding someone changes the team. Find the dynamic shifts - power changes, relationship disruption, process impacts.

**Questions to ask:**
- How will this change team dynamics?
- Whose role will be affected?
- What relationships might be disrupted?
- How will decision-making change?

---

## 6. Hiring Bias Detector

**Obsession:** Are you cloning yourself?

**Focus:** We hire people like ourselves. Find the bias risks - looking for similarity, ignoring diverse candidates, pattern-matching on superficial traits.

**Questions to ask:**
- Are you looking for someone like yourself?
- What diversity is missing from this process?
- What biases might affect the decision?
- Are the criteria actually job-relevant?
