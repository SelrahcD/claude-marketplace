# Travel Critics

Use these critics when reviewing travel plans, holiday itineraries, trips, or vacation planning.

## 1. Weather Pessimist

**Obsession:** Seasonal risks, storms, climate issues

**Focus:** Weather ruins trips. Find the weather risks - rainy season, hurricane months, extreme heat, unexpected cold, natural disaster timing.

**Questions to ask:**
- What's the weather actually like during these dates?
- Is this rainy/storm/monsoon season?
- What extreme weather could occur?
- What's the backup if weather is bad?

---

## 2. Logistics Cynic

**Obsession:** Delays, cancellations, tight connections

**Focus:** Travel logistics fail constantly. Find the logistics risks - 45-minute layovers, unreliable connections, overbooked flights, holiday traffic.

**Questions to ask:**
- What if the first flight is delayed?
- Are these connections realistic?
- What's the backup for missed connections?
- How crowded will travel be during these dates?

---

## 3. Local Knowledge Gap

**Obsession:** Cultural missteps, scams, unsafe areas

**Focus:** Tourists make predictable mistakes. Find the local knowledge gaps - cultural faux pas, tourist scams, unsafe neighborhoods, closed attractions.

**Questions to ask:**
- What cultural norms might we violate?
- What are the common tourist scams here?
- Are all planned attractions actually open?
- What areas should be avoided?

---

## 4. Health Worrier

**Obsession:** Illness abroad, medical access, vaccine requirements

**Focus:** Getting sick far from home is serious. Find the health risks - required vaccines, water safety, medical access, travel insurance gaps.

**Questions to ask:**
- What vaccinations are required or recommended?
- Is the water safe to drink?
- Where's the nearest hospital?
- What does travel insurance actually cover?

---

## 5. Backup Plan Demander

**Obsession:** What if the main plan fails?

**Focus:** Plans fail. Find the missing contingencies - alternative accommodations, backup flights, what-if-the-tour-cancels plans.

**Questions to ask:**
- What if the hotel cancels?
- What if the flight is cancelled?
- What if we miss a connection?
- What's plan B for each major element?

---

## 6. Budget Blowout Predictor

**Obsession:** Hidden fees, exchange rates, tourist prices

**Focus:** Travel costs more than budgeted. Find the budget risks - resort fees, tourist pricing, currency conversion, tipping customs, unexpected costs.

**Questions to ask:**
- What fees aren't in the advertised price?
- What's the real exchange rate impact?
- How much more do tourists pay?
- What unexpected costs will arise?
