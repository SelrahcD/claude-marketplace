# Purchase Critics

Use these critics when reviewing major purchases, buying decisions, equipment investments, or significant acquisitions.

## 1. Total Cost of Ownership

**Obsession:** Maintenance, insurance, taxes, repairs

**Focus:** Purchase price is just the beginning. Find the ongoing costs - maintenance, insurance, taxes, repairs, consumables, storage.

**Questions to ask:**
- What are the ongoing costs?
- What maintenance is required?
- What insurance and taxes apply?
- What's the true cost over 5 years?

---

## 2. Depreciation Doomsayer

**Obsession:** How fast does this lose value?

**Focus:** Most purchases lose value. Find the depreciation risks - rapid value loss, obsolescence, wear patterns, market changes.

**Questions to ask:**
- How fast does this depreciate?
- What's it worth in a year? Five years?
- Is this technology about to become obsolete?
- What will make this worthless?

---

## 3. Lifestyle Fit Skeptic

**Obsession:** Does this match how you actually live?

**Focus:** We buy for imagined lives, not real ones. Find the lifestyle mismatch - features you won't use, requirements you can't meet, changes you won't make.

**Questions to ask:**
- Will you actually use this as intended?
- Does this fit your real life or imagined life?
- What lifestyle changes does this require?
- What features will you never use?

---

## 4. Alternative Investigator

**Obsession:** What else could this money do?

**Focus:** Every purchase has alternatives. Find the alternatives - rent vs buy, different products, different price points, not buying at all.

**Questions to ask:**
- What alternatives exist?
- Could you rent instead of buy?
- What if you spent half this amount?
- What if you didn't buy this at all?

---

## 5. Resale Reality Checker

**Obsession:** Can you get out if needed?

**Focus:** Exit matters. Find the resale risks - illiquid markets, customization that kills resale, timing dependencies.

**Questions to ask:**
- How easy is this to sell?
- What's the resale market like?
- What reduces resale value?
- Can you exit this purchase if needed?

---

## 6. Impulse Purchase Detector

**Obsession:** Are you buying emotionally?

**Focus:** Emotions drive purchases. Find the emotional drivers - FOMO, status seeking, retail therapy, pressure tactics.

**Questions to ask:**
- Why do you want this right now?
- What emotional need is this filling?
- Would you still want this in 30 days?
- Are you being pressured or manipulated?
